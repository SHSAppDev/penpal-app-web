About the Founders

We are three programmers that collaborated on this idea on how we could make this website called World Without Walls. The purpose of the website is to make an instant message penpal with special tools like the ability to translate words or phrases,speech to text, and grammar/spelling coreection.We came up with the idea for the website and ran with it, to develop the messaging website known as World Without Walls.
